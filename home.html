LESSONS LEARNED
Improve chip and coolant shielding around tool taper New

In earlier builds, debris ingress at the tool holder taper led to increased runout and maintenance downtime. Adding shielding mitigates this issue.

KEY TAKEAWAYS
  • Ensure adequate chip flow clearance near taper
  • Position coolant flow lines not backward with holder seal
  • Use gaskets at flange to keep chips from interfering
Systems Engineer We should isolate functional impacts and ensure it's a good machined reference face early in the stack to reduce tolerance accumulation.
ReviewAI Add a locking or clamping mechanism to protect the tool taper from chip ingress.
