Learn from past mistakes automatically

When lessons learned are buried in old files or forgotten projects, it's easy to repeat the same mistakes. CoLab's AutoReview helps teams surface similar past issues during your current review. So, you can catch known risks before they become production problems.

Better feedback from more experts

Design reviews only work when the right people weigh in. CoLab makes it easy to bring in the right voices at the right time. You can assign reviewers, tag teammates in context, and track who's seen what. So, nothing gets missed or delayed.

Iterate faster with AI-powered design checks

Engineers spend too much time reviewing low-level design issues. CoLab's AutoReview generates comments based on your team's past feedback and design checklists—so common problems get flagged automatically. Each comment is pinned to the model, with clear context on what to fix and why it matters. So, engineers can focus on impactful decisions, not basic checks.

Track every issue to resolution

Design feedback shouldn't disappear into inboxes or get lost in notes. CoLab automatically logs and tracks every review issue. So, reviews stay on track and no issues fall through the cracks. Teams who use CoLab follow through on more issues and avoid repeat mistakes.
